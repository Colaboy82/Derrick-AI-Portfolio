Personal Portfolio Site

A personal site designed to present work, interests, and personality in a way that feels clearer and more human than a static resume alone.

Goal

Build a portfolio that makes experience easy to scan while also showing what you are currently interested in professionally and personally.

Design Direction

Use a dark, modern interface with strong contrast, clean cards, smooth hover states, and a small number of focused sections.

Why It Matters

The project acts as both a communication tool and a sandbox for refining how your background, projects, and interests are presented online.
